Mail Services


Servers will likely have multiple ports for diff mail related services (e.g SMTP, IMAP, etc).

Creds may work for 1 but not for others.

There are also Web Interfaces (e.g. Round Cube) for some Mail services.

Dovecot

doveadm is Dovecot's administration utility, a command-line tool used to manage and interact with the Dovecot mail server.

It allows for various mailbox operations, including moving, copying, fetching, and managing flags, and can also be used to manage quota and other Dovecot settings.

For example, doveadm can let you read Dovecot messages (which are in binary files).
